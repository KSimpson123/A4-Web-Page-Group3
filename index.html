Welcome

Haunted Washington

Haunted Washington is the Pacific Northwest’s premier destination for all things spooky! We offer many tours of local haunts, perfect for your next terrifying adventure.

Are you tired of learning about the paranormal from the relative safety and comfort of your home? Do you secretly long to be led through dark, dilapidated buildings and vast, forested graveyards? Part of the fun of a Haunted Washington tour is the thrill of embracing the unknown. Will all parties in your group safely return? Only fate shall decide.

Perfect for both skeptics and paranormal believers, we offer various unearthly tours suited to your group’s needs. From bachelorette parties and baby showers, to corporate events or a “ghouls” night out, our highly trained tour guides will professionally terrorize you to your core.

About Us

We are a Seattle-based group of web developers who have had the pleasure of traveling all over Washington. In celebration of this year’s “Spooky Season”, we decided to develop a site where you can explore some of our favorite haunted places from behind your screen. Our group has a variety of experience in web development, ranging from HTML and CSS to Python. It was a pleasure to collaborate with each other and create this eerie webpage for you all. We hope you enjoy it, Happy Halloween!
